01. Technical Philosophy

PERCS is engineered on the principle of Zero-Persistence. We believe that privacy is not a promise, but a mathematical certainty. Our infrastructure is designed to make the collection of user data technically impossible.

02. Non-Existent Telemetry

We do not collect, view, or store the following data points:

  • Network Identifiers: No IP addresses are logged. Edge nodes operate on diskless RAM-only systems that purge transit metadata every 60 seconds.
  • Device Fingerprints: We do not track User-Agents, screen resolutions, OS versions, or hardware IDs.
  • Traffic Analysis: We do not perform Deep Packet Inspection (DPI). Your traffic is encrypted and passed through a 3-hop obfuscation matrix.
THE 69-NODE GUARANTEE: Our network utilizes 69 memory-resident nodes across 4 high-privacy jurisdictions (Switzerland, Iceland, Seychelles, and Singapore). Data never touches a physical hard drive during transit.

03. Vault Security & Metadata

When you utilize The Vault (1.7TB storage matrix), your privacy is protected by our automated forensic pipeline:

  • Automated Sanitization: All uploads are automatically stripped of EXIF, GPS, device signatures, and XML metadata.
  • Cryptographic Sharding: Files are split into 16 shards and distributed globally. No single server possesses a readable file.
  • Zero-Knowledge Access: We do not hold the keys to your shards. Only your cryptographic handle can reassemble your data.
