Privacy Policy

01. AI Moderation Data

Our Automated Content Filters process message data in volatile RAM. No plaintext message content is ever written to our permanent database disks. Sentiment scores are calculated locally and discarded once the moderation action is complete.

02. Collected Metadata

  • Cryptographic Hashes: Used for tracking economy balances and level progress.
