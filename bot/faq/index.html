Bot FAQ

Everything you need to know about integrating PERCS into your server environment.

What is the default prefix?

PERCS uses . as the default prefix for text commands. However, most commands are also available via / slash commands for a more integrated Discord experience.

How do I use the economy system?

Simply type .work or .daily to begin. You can view your current balance with .bal or check the local rankings with .lb.

Is my data private?

Yes. PERCS follows a zero-persistence protocol. We do not store message content or private user data beyond what is required for functional features like XP or Economy balances.

How do I report a bug?

The best way to report issues is through our official Discord server. Our development team monitors the support channels 24/7 to resolve critical protocol bugs.
